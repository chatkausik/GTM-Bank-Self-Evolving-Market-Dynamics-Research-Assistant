⚡ What is GTM Product Analyzer Agent?
A self-evolving AI research assistant that learns from both successes and failures. It surfaces strategic insights grounded in your actual launch history — not generic advice.
Core moat: Competitors optimize from wins only. GTM Product Analyzer Agent systematically mines why products underperformed.
